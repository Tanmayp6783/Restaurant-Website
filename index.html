Maharaja Bhog

Maharaja Bhog in the Veg Aroma in Jalgaon for their wholesome Gujarati and Rajasthani thali. Simply sit back as the servers keep pouring in authentic delicacies and feast!

Their vegetarian premium thali includes three appetizers, four types of vegetables, two types of dal, three types of Indian breads, two types of rice and two desserts.

Varan Batti

Varan Baati is an Indian dish of daal and baati. It is popular Maharashtra's Khandesh and Vidarbha region.

Daal is prepared using tuvaar dal & Baati is a hard bread made up of wheat flour commonly known as aata which is very healthy, tasty & easy to digest.

King of Street food

Misal pav is a popular Maharashtrian street food of usal topped with onions, tomatoes, lemon juice, coriander leaves and served with a side of soft pav.

This misal recipe is a tasty and filling vegan dish that can be served as breakfast, lunch or brunch.

Red Kidney beans in spiced gravy

The aromatic blend of red kidney beans simmered in a flavourful onion & tomato-based gravy, which is paired with fluffy & steamed rice that creates a comforting harmony of taste and texture.

It's not just a meal,it's a warm embrace, bringing solace and contentment

Taco Bell Copycat

This vegetarian crunchwrap supreme swaps the beef for seasoned lentils and adds mashed avocado for a flavor boost.

It is crunchy, chewy, soft and super delicious! Each bite feels like a party in your mouth with so many different flavors and textures.
